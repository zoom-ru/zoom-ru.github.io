    Sustainability & Impact at Zoom

    Delivering happiness and positive impacts through our platform and people.

    Zoom is guided by our core value of Care and our mission to connect people across the globe. We care for our amazing communities, customers and teammates and we invite you to learn more about our story in our Impact Report, as we share the innovative evolution of our product, sustainability program and social impact work.

    Kelly Steckelberg Chief Financial Officer

    This year’s Impact Report brings together our company strategy, sustainability, social impact, and diversity, equity and inclusion efforts into a single report for Zoom’s fiscal year 2024.

    We care for our company

    With the continued urgency of the climate crisis, we are proud to provide a secure and seamless platform that allows people to communicate, connect and collaborate virtually, and ultimately contribute to the global effort to reduce emissions.

    Real estate portfolio

    Targeting LEED Certification for new office spaces

    Data centers

    Global renewable energy coverage of 94.5% with our Equinix data centers

    Compliance and ethics

    Continuing to support a strong Speak Up culture and freedom of expression

    Nonprofit feature: 350 Pacific Climate Warriors

    350 Pacific Climate Warriors is a Zoom Cares grant partner who fights climate change by providing solar panel education for island communities.

    We care for our customers

    We are excited about our evolution and our dedication to expanding the Zoom Workplace platform to advance collaboration and deliver limitless human connection.

    Artificial intelligence

    A federated, responsible and empowering approach to AI

    Who uses Zoom

    85% of Forbes Cloud 100 and 45 out of the top 50 of the Fortune World's Most Admired Companies choose Zoom

    Data privacy and security

    Certifications, external research and ample planning ensure customer data is protected and safe

    Customer feature: MLB

    Major League Baseball™ re-envisions the fan experience via the Zoom Replay Operations Center to provide viewers instant access to real-time field decisions like never before.

    We care for our communities

    Zoom Cares, the global social impact arm of Zoom, leverages Zoom’s resources to address the issues facing our communities in the areas of innovative learning, climate solutions, youth mental health, and social equity.

    Philanthropy

    Donated $3,775,000 in grant funding to 50 organizations in 20 countries

    Product donations

    Donated $1.7 million worth of in-kind licenses to 70 organizations in 14 countries

    Employee giving

    Since 2020, employees and Zoom have contributed more than $3 million in employee-led giving, serving 2,600 nonprofits worldwide

    Nonprofit feature: Re:Coded

    The Zoom and Re:Coded partnership helps remove systemic barriers and supports displaced and underserved talent to reskill and build sustainable careers in the digital economy.

    We care for our teammates

    We embrace programs designed to help employees feel comfortable, safe, and connected in their role, and within Zoom as a whole.

    Supporting one another

    9 employee resource groups for connection with peers

    Listening to our Zoomies’ voices

    91% participation rate in our employee engagement survey

    Benefits and support

    Above-market compensation programs, wellness resources, flexible working options and robust career development

    Volunteering Feature: Global Day of Care

    Over 800 Zoomies in over 80 cities dedicated their time to volunteering with local nonprofits for Zoom’s inaugural Global Day of Care

    Read more Read more

    We care for ourselves

    Care is our core value, and we embody this value as we support the health and wellbeing of Zoomies.

    Support for Zoomies

    Over 20 virtual support programs for employees

     

     

    Zoomies Helping Zoomies

    Launched Zoomies Helping Zoomies fund in partnership with E4E Relief. The Fund provides financial support to employees facing extraordinary personal crises resulting from unfortunate events and/or urgent personal hardships

    Product donations

    Communicates with and supports employees during uncertain times and catastrophic events
